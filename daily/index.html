Welcome to Subjectle!

It’s a Wordle clone with a twist.

Your goal is to guess the correct student based on their class combinations.

After each guess, the game gives you feedback by way of colours.

Yellow means the class is correct but with a different teacher or line.

Green means your guess is exactly right.

You have 6 tries to find the right student.

Each guess helps you narrow down the possibilities.
